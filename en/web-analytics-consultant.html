Definition

A cybersecurity engineer is an information security professional that performs many functions including designing, developing and implementing secure network solutions to defend against advanced cyberattacks, hacking and persistent threats.Information systems security engineers (ISSE), help businesses keep sensitive data safe. Data may include confidential client records such as patient records at a hospital, trade secrets, and financial records that are all potential targets from cyber-attacks. Being an ISSE plays important roles in guarding businesses, government, and individuals against hackers and cyber-criminals who are always creating new ways to infiltrate sensitive databases
